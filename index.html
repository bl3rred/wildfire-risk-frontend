 Key Calculations

                

                    

                        
 NDVI

                        
Normalized Difference Vegetation Index

                        
Measures vegetation health: (NIR - Red) / (NIR + Red)

                        Higher values = more vegetation
                    

                    

                        
 Temperature

                        
Land Surface Temperature

                        
From MODIS satellite, indicates heat stress

                        Higher temps = increased fire risk
                    

                    

                        
 Risk Scoring

                        
Neural Network Prediction

                        
Model trained on historical wildfire/flood data with Focal Loss

                        83% overall accuracy
